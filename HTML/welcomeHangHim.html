Rules

The game consists of finding a word by guessing the letters that make it up. You have the right to make only 8 errors. On this first page you only need to enter your username and your e-mail adress.

Warning: the e-mail have to be a priplanussian mail for example: \ddddd[sssss|sssss]

On the next page, you will have to choose your level of game and generate a word by clicking on a button. Enjoy !
